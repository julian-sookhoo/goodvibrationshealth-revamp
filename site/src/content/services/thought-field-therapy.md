## What Is TFT?

Thought Field Therapy (TFT) is a drug-free, non-invasive technique that uses a specific sequence of tapping on meridian points to balance the body's energy system and neutralize the emotional or physical charge behind a problem. The technique works by interrupting the thought field — the energy pattern activated when you think about a troubling issue — and eliminating its ability to generate a negative response.

Developed by Dr. Roger Callahan, TFT is one of the original energy psychology methods. Fern Wolf trained directly to the TFT-Advanced level and has been certified since 2013.

## Who Is This For?

TFT is effective for a wide range of conditions:

- Anxiety and panic attacks
- Phobias and fears
- Trauma and PTSD
- Grief and loss
- Depression
- Chronic and acute physical pain
- Anger and guilt
- Addictive urges and compulsive behaviour
- Insomnia
- Self-sabotage
- Weight management challenges
- Obsessive thoughts

## What to Expect in a Session

1. You'll bring to mind the issue you want to address — you don't need to describe it in detail
2. Fern will guide you through a specific tapping algorithm on meridian points (face, hand, and chest)
3. You'll rate your distress level (0–10) before and after
4. Most people notice a significant drop — often to zero — within one or a few rounds
5. Results typically hold without maintenance

Sessions are conducted virtually via video call or phone.
