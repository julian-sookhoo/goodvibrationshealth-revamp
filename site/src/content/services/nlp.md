## What Is NLP?

Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) is a set of techniques that explores the connection between neurological processes (how you think), language (how you talk to yourself and others), and behavioural patterns learned through experience (what you do automatically). By working at the intersection of these three, NLP offers a fast and practical path to changing thoughts, responses, and habits.

Unlike hypnosis, NLP works in a fully conscious, conversational state — using specific language patterns, reframing techniques, and perceptual exercises to create new mental pathways.

Fern Wolf is a Certified NLP Practitioner.

## Who Is This For?

NLP works well for:

- Low self-confidence or negative self-talk
- Mood swings and emotional reactivity
- Specific phobias
- Depression and anxiety
- Stress management
- Communication challenges (in relationships or at work)
- Performance (sports, public speaking, professional)
- Habit change

## What to Expect in a Session

1. We'll identify the specific pattern, response, or outcome you want to change
2. Using NLP techniques (reframing, anchoring, timeline work, etc.), we'll shift the underlying structure of the problem
3. You'll leave with practical tools you can use on your own

Sessions are active and conversational — no extended silence or relaxation required.
