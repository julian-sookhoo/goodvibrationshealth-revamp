## What Is Psych-K?

Psych-K® is a fast, spiritually grounded set of techniques for rewriting the limiting beliefs stored in the subconscious mind. Developed by Rob Williams, it draws from contemporary neuroscience, kinesiology, and ancient wisdom traditions. It uses whole-brain integration — allowing both hemispheres to work together — to install new, supportive beliefs at the deepest level.

Fern Wolf completed Psych-K certification in 2005 (both Basic and Advanced workshops) and has been working with this method for over 20 years.

## Who Is This For?

If you recognize yourself in any of these, Psych-K may be exactly right for you:

- "I'm not good enough / smart enough / worthy enough"
- "I don't deserve to be healthy / wealthy / loved"
- "I always self-sabotage when things are going well"
- "No matter how hard I try, I can't seem to change"
- "I trust my logic but my feelings override it"
- Persistent patterns that return even after therapy or coaching

## What to Expect in a Session

1. Describe the situation or pattern you want to change
2. Identify the specific limiting belief driving it (with Fern's guidance)
3. Co-create a new, supportive belief to replace it
4. Use a whole-brain state process to install the new belief at the subconscious level
5. Verify the change using muscle testing

Many clients notice an immediate shift in how they feel about the issue — thoughts feel different, lighter, more open.
