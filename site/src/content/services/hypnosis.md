## What Is Hypnosis?

Hypnosis is a natural state of focused attention that allows a practitioner to work directly with the subconscious mind — where habits, beliefs, and automatic responses are stored. Contrary to the stage-show image, clinical hypnosis is a gentle, collaborative process. You remain fully aware and in control throughout.

Fern uses the **Simpson Protocol** — an advanced method that communicates directly with the superconscious mind, without requiring the client to verbalize their issue or identify its root cause. This makes it particularly effective for issues that feel too private, too complex, or too undefined to articulate.

Fern holds three hypnosis certifications: Certified Hypnotist (National Guild of Hypnotists), Simpson Protocol, and Virtual Gastric Band Hypnosis.
