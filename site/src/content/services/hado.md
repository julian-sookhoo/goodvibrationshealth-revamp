## What Is HADO?

HADO is a practice rooted in the groundbreaking research of Dr. Masaru Emoto, who demonstrated that water responds to human intention, words, and music at the molecular level. His research — widely known through the book and documentary *What the Bleep Do We Know?* — showed that water exposed to positive language, music, and intention forms beautiful crystalline structures.

HADO applies this principle to your daily water practice, using written intention and positive focus to support wellbeing.

Fern Wolf trained directly under Dr. Masaru Emoto and holds a Hado Instructor certification.

## Who Is This For?

HADO is offered as a complementary wellness practice for clients who:

- Want a simple, daily supportive practice at home
- Are interested in the relationship between consciousness and physical matter
- Are using other therapeutic modalities and want ongoing energetic support between sessions
- Resonate with intention-based, mind-body approaches

## What to Expect

1. Use a glass bottle of pure, filtered water
2. Write positive intention statements and affix them to the bottle (facing inward)
3. Keep the bottle away from electronic devices
4. Drink the water throughout the day

Fern guides clients through personalizing their intention practice to align with their specific wellness goals.
