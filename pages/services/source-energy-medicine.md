## What Is Source Energy Medicine?
Source Energy Medicine® was developed by Stephen Pollitt and is grounded in the understanding that water carries and transmits vibrational information. Through a personalized process, your specific wellness intentions are encoded into water you drink daily — creating a consistent vibrational anchor for the changes you want to see.

Fern Wolf is the Canadian Representative for Source Energy Medicine.

## Who Is This For?
Source Energy Medicine is used as a complementary support practice for clients who want:

- A daily, ongoing energetic practice between sessions
- Support for physical, mental, emotional, or spiritual wellbeing
- A framework for setting clear, specific healing intentions
- Something tangible to use at home

It is often used alongside other modalities — TFT, Psych-K, hypnosis — as a daily support between sessions.

## What to Expect
The process involves:
1. Working with Fern to write a detailed, specific description of your wellness intentions
2. Creating a personalized label for your water bottle
3. Drinking the charged water daily as an ongoing practice

A free Quick Start Guide is available online to introduce the approach.

## FAQ

**Q: Is this a medical treatment?**
A: No. Source Energy Medicine is a wellness practice, not a medical treatment. It is not a substitute for professional medical care.

**Q: How does this work virtually?**
A: The process is entirely virtual. Fern guides you through setting your intentions and creating your personalized practice via video call.

**Q: Do I need any special equipment?**
A: Only a glass water bottle and water. Everything else is provided as part of the session.
