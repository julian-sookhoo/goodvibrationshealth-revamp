## What Is TFT?
Thought Field Therapy (TFT) is a drug-free, non-invasive technique that uses a specific sequence of tapping on meridian points to balance the body's energy system and neutralize the emotional or physical charge behind a problem. The technique works by interrupting the thought field — the energy pattern activated when you think about a troubling issue — and eliminating its ability to generate a negative response.

Developed by Dr. Roger Callahan, TFT is one of the original energy psychology methods. Fern Wolf trained directly to the TFT-Advanced level and has been certified since 2013.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I have to talk about the details of my trauma or issue?**
A: No. TFT requires you to think about the issue, but you don't need to verbalize or describe what it is. The technique works without disclosure.

**Q: How many sessions will I need?**
A: Many specific issues (a particular fear, a specific traumatic memory) resolve in a single session. Complex or layered issues may benefit from multiple sessions.

**Q: Does it work virtually?**
A: Yes, fully. TFT is equally effective by phone or video — the practitioner guides your tapping, and you do it yourself in your own space.

**Q: Is this the same as EFT (Emotional Freedom Techniques)?**
A: TFT is the original tapping therapy — EFT was developed later, based on TFT principles. TFT uses specific, condition-targeted algorithms rather than general tapping sequences.
