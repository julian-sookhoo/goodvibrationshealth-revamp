## About the Services

**Q: Are these services safe?**
A: Yes. Every modality I offer is non-invasive and drug-free. They are gentle enough to work alongside any existing medical treatment and carry no known harmful side effects.

**Q: How is this different from seeing a doctor or therapist?**
A: Conventional medicine and therapy focus primarily on managing symptoms or working through issues consciously. My work targets the subconscious root of problems — the beliefs, emotional charges, and energy patterns that drive symptoms in the first place. For many clients, the two approaches complement each other beautifully.

**Q: Can I use these services alongside my current medical treatment?**
A: Absolutely. These services are not a replacement for medical care — they work alongside it. Always follow your doctor's advice for medical conditions. Many clients find that our work accelerates the results they're already pursuing.

**Q: How many sessions will I need?**
A: This varies by person and issue. Some clients experience complete resolution of a specific problem in a single session. Others benefit from a series of sessions for deeper, layered issues. I'll give you an honest assessment after your first appointment.

**Q: What if I'm skeptical?**
A: Healthy skepticism is welcome here. You don't need to believe in a technique for it to work — you just need to be willing to try it. I'd invite you to book a free discovery call, tell me what you're dealing with, and let the results speak for themselves.

**Q: Which service is right for me?**
A: Browse [by condition](/help/) or [by service category](/services/). If you're still unsure, a [free 30-minute discovery call](/book/) is the easiest way to find out — I'll ask a few questions and recommend the best starting point for your situation.
